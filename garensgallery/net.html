Net Art

My website represents a story about a son and his dad. The website contains multiple images and some sound effects. With the website I wanted to tell a story that has been already told in a different form that it's original state. The site itself is a bit of a puzzle that'll loop back if you don't go through the right links. Explore it and look for what can be clicked on with the mouse, the final page will redirect you somewhere else so if you don't get to that you haven't seen the whole site.
